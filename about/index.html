ABOUT ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Luuk Noverraz, 18 years old. Currently I'm studying Communication and Multimedia Design at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences or the HvA. Previously I've learned to code over the course of 6 years at the Metis Montessori Lyceum's Coderclass. I'm a developer and designer in various fields and most of all interested in learning and creating.

Besides coding and designing, I'm also interested in video editing, gaming, writing, history and more. Over the years, I've built solid habits with regard to planned working including Git and scrum. If you are interested in contacting me, don't hesitate to reach out or send me an email.

My coding abilities lie mainly in the following areas:

C# for use in game development. For web development, I use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React and when required I can work with third-party tools as well. Online store management with Liquid. For general purposes, I am also experienced in using Python. Furthermore, I have worked with several languages relevent to data visualization such as R and pandas.

I use a multitude of appliances in my workflow. In the field of game dev, I have experience with mainly Unity but have used Unreal and GameMaker Studio as well. For 3D modeling and animations, I use Blender.

For my design work, I mainly use Affinity Designer, though I have worked with Adobe Photoshop as well. I also have years of experience working with Adobe Premiere Pro for video editing projects.

About me

Hello everyone, my name is Luuk Noverraz. I'm a 14 year old fourth year student at the Metis Montessori Lyceum, in the Coderclass to be precise. My hobbies and passions consist of graphic design, web development and gaming, which includes game design and development. I also like video editing, history, and taking care of my guinea pigs and cat. I'm also part of the Robotics club at my school, and have participated in multiple competitions, including the First Lego League and the WRO. Beyond this, I've worked on several projects in the Coderclass, which you're able to find at the projects page.

To see my design work, you can go to my Dribbble page in the bottom right corner. I'd love to work on even more awesome projects, so if you want to ask me something, be sure to send me an e-mail at luuknoverraz@gmail.com.
